Good Questions

Frequently asked questions

What types of appliances do you repair?+
All the household workhorses: refrigerators and freezers, ice machines, ovens and stoves, dishwashers, washers and dryers, vent hoods, and garbage disposals — for both homes and businesses.
Do you service all appliance brands?+
Yes. Our technicians work on every major brand — Samsung, LG, GE, Whirlpool, Frigidaire, Bosch, KitchenAid, Maytag, JennAir, Amana, Electrolux, Miele and more.
How quickly can you respond to a service request?+
Same-day service is our standard whenever the schedule allows — and we're open until 8 PM Monday through Friday, plus Saturdays. Call in the morning and in most cases we can have a technician at your door that day.
Do you sell appliance parts too?+
Yes — we run a full parts store at 3701 Apollo Rd with OEM and aftermarket parts for every major brand, backed by our Marcone partnership for fast availability. Call the parts counter directly at (361) 400-9513.
Are your technicians certified and insured?+
Yes — every technician is certified and fully insured, and our field techs stay in contact with a master technician for tougher diagnoses.
Do you offer a warranty on your repairs?+
We stand behind our work with a warranty on every repair. If something isn't right, we make it right.
Can you handle both residential and commercial repairs?+
Yes — we service homes as well as restaurants and businesses across the Coastal Bend, including commercial refrigeration and ice machines.
How can I schedule a service appointment?+
Call or text (361) 673-0937, or send the request form below — we'll confirm your appointment right away. Open Mon–Fri 8 AM–8 PM, Sat 9 AM–4 PM.
