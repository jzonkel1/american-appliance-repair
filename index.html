🔥 Good Questions

Frequently asked questions

What types of appliances do you repair?+
We repair all the household workhorses: refrigerators and freezers, ovens and stoves, dishwashers, washers and dryers, vent hoods, and garbage disposals — for both homes and businesses.
Do you service all appliance brands?+
Yes. Our technicians work on every major brand — Samsung, LG, GE, Whirlpool, Frigidaire, Bosch, Amana, Electrolux, Miele and more. If it's in your kitchen or laundry room, we can service it.
How quickly can you respond to a service request?+
Same-day service is our standard whenever the schedule allows. Call in the morning and in most cases we can have a technician at your door that day.
Are your technicians certified and insured?+
Yes — every technician is certified and fully insured, and our field techs stay in contact with a master technician for tougher diagnoses.
Do you offer a warranty on your repairs?+
We stand behind our work with a warranty on every repair. If something isn't right, we make it right.
Are your repair services affordable?+
You get up-front, transparent pricing before any work begins — no surprises. Customers regularly tell us the bill came in under what they expected to pay.
Can you handle both residential and commercial repairs?+
Yes — we service homes as well as businesses across the Coastal Bend, and we stock parts for both at our Apollo Rd shop.
How can I schedule a service appointment?+
Call or text (361) 673-0937, or send the request form below — we'll confirm your appointment right away.
